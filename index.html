Recipe: How to cook rice for 4

Ingredients

  1. 1 kg of rice
  2. half cup of cooking oil
  3. 1 spoon of salt
  4. 2 litres of water
  5. 4 pieces of carrot
  6. 1 garlic
  7. 1 teaspoon of ginger powder
  8. 1 teaspoon of pilau masala

Procedure

  1. Pour the water into a cooking pot
  2. Add salt, cooking oil, ginger powder and pilau masala to the water and stir
  3. Create the carrots and add then to the mixture
  4. Chop the garlic and add it to the mixture
  5. Stir the mixture
  6. Boil the mixture
  7. While boiling wash the rice and add it to the pot
  8. Stir the mixture for 1 minute
  9. Continue cooking for 30 minutes with medium heat
  10. After 30 minutes of cooking the rice is ready

Serving

You can serve a plate of rice with a bowl of beef stew, vegetables and a beverage of your choice
